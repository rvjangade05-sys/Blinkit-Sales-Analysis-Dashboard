# 🛒 Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is a **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze Blinkit's sales data. It provides insights into **sales performance, product categories, outlet types, and customer behavior** using interactive visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Track overall sales performance
* Analyze product categories and item types
* Compare outlet performance (Tier & Size)
* Understand customer ratings and preferences
* Identify business growth opportunities

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

### 🔑 Key Metrics

* 💰 **Total Sales:** $1.20M
* 📦 **Number of Items:** 8523
* 📊 **Average Sales:** $141
* ⭐ **Average Rating:** 3.9

---

### 📈 Visual Insights

#### 🏬 Outlet Establishment Trend

* Sales increased significantly over time
* Peak sales reached around **$205K**
* Indicates strong business growth in recent years

---

#### 🛍️ Item Type Analysis

* Top performing categories:

  * Fruits & Snacks (~$0.18M each)
  * Household & Frozen items
* Low-performing categories:

  * Seafood, Breakfast

---

#### 🥗 Fat Content Analysis

* **Regular items generate higher revenue** compared to low-fat items
* Helps understand customer consumption patterns

---

#### 🏪 Outlet Size Distribution

* Medium-size outlets contribute the highest sales (~$507K)
* Followed by small and high-size outlets

---

#### 📍 Outlet Location (Tier-wise)

* **Tier 3 cities lead in sales (~472K)**
* Tier 2 and Tier 1 follow respectively
* Shows strong demand in smaller cities

---

#### 🏬 Outlet Type Performance

| Outlet Type       | Total Sales | No. of Items | Avg Sales | Rating |
